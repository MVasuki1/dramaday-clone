Big Mouth

Title: 빅마우스 / Big Mouth
Genre: Legal, Crime
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: MBC
Broadcast period: 2022-July-29 to 2022-Sep-17
Air time: Friday & Saturday 21:50

Story of a lawyer with a 10% win rate who accidentally takes on a murder case, where he discovers the conspiracy within privileged class. Overnight, he becomes the rare genius con artist “Big Mouth/Big Mouse”. He aims to uncover the real face of the priviliged class to protect his family and to survive.
