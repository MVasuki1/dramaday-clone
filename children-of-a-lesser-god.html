Children of a Lesser God

Title: 작은 신의 아이들 / Children of a Lesser God
Genre: Supernatural, Mystery
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: OCN
Broadcast period: 2018-March-03 to 2018-April-22
Air time: Saturday & Sunday 22:00 KST

Cheon Jae In (Kang Ji Hwan) is a detective and he is extremely smart. He is guided by only facts, logic and numbers. Kim Dan ( Kim Ok Bin) is a detective who possesses a superhuman ability involving her vision. They are totally different in personality, but they work together to uncover a conspiracy involving a powerful organization.
