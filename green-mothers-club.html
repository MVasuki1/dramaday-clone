Green Mothers’ Club

Title: 그린마더스클럽 / Green Mother’s Club
Genre: Friendship
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: jTBC
Broadcast period: 2022-April-06 to 2022-May-26
Air time: Wednesday & Thursday 22:30 KST

A story depicting the friendships, motherly love, and growth of 5 mothers.

The mothers, who all have elementary school age children, meet around the school. The have their own complexes, which they are unable to get over. By interacting with each other, the mothers’ friendships develop and they grow as people.
