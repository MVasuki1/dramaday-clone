The Ghost Detective

Title: 오늘의 탐정 / The Ghost Detective
Also known as: Today’s Detective / Today’s Private Investigator
Genre: Horror, Thriller, Mystery
Episodes: 32
Broadcast network: KBS2
Broadcast period: 2018-Sep-05 to 2018-Oct-31
Air time: Wednesday & Thursday 22:00 KST

This drama tell the story of a detective, Lee Da Il (Choi Daniel) who catches ghosts, tries to solve the case of his younger sibling’s bizarre death with the help of his assistant Jung Yeo Wool (Park Eun Bin). He runs into a mysterious woman called Seon Woo Hye (Lee Ji Ah) who appears at every crime scene.
