Crazy Love

Title: 크레이지 러브 / Crazy Love
Genre: Romance, Comedy
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: KBS2
Broadcast period: 2022-Mar-07 to 2022-Apr-26
Air time: Monday & Tuesday 21:30 KST

Crazy Love tells the love story of Noh Go Jin (Kim Jae Wook), the CEO of GOTOP which is South Korea top math institute, pretending to have amnesia after receiving death threats, and Lee Shin Ah (Krystal Jung), Go Jin’s introvert and quiet secretary, who doesn’t have much time left. Shin-ah later pretends to be the fiance of Go Jin knowing he has amnesia.
