The Goddess of Revenge

Title: 복수해라 / The Goddess of Revenge
Also known as: Get Revenge
Genre: Melodrama, Mystery, Revenge
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: CSTV
Broadcast period: 2020-Nov-21 to 2021-Jan-17
Air time: Saturday & Sunday 21:00 KST

This drama tells the revenge story Of Kang Hae Ra (Kim Sa Rang) against those who rule society. She is a trendy influencer reporter until a phony scandal ruins her. She vows a revenge on those who hurt her.

Meanwhile, Cha Min Joon (Yoon Hyun Min) is a cold-blooded lawyer with a 100% win rate. After his family was trapped in a scheme and ruined, he only thinks about his success and getting revenge for his family.
