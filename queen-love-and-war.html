Queen: Love And War

Title: 간택-여인들의 전쟁 / Queen: Love And War
Also known as: Selection: The War Between Women
Genre: Fantasy, Historical
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: CSTV
Broadcast period: 2019-Dec-14 to 2020-Feb-09
Air time: Air time: Saturday & Sunday 22:50 KST

Kang Eun Bo’s (Jin Se Yun) twin sister is murdered. To find out the who is responsible for her sister’s death, Kang Eun Bo decides to become the queen. She does not hesitate to take reckless actions in pursuit of her goal. Meanwhile, Lee Kyung (Kim Min Kyu) is the king. He is handsome and intelligent. Lee Kyung has precognitive dreams about one woman. He becomes involved in an unexpected case.
