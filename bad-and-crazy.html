Bad and Crazy

Title: 배드 앤 크레이지 / Bad and Crazy
Genre: Action, Fantasy, Detective
Episodes: 12
Broadcast network: tvN
Broadcast period: 2021-Dec-17 to 2022-Jan-28
Air time: Friday & Saturday 22:40 KST

Soo-yeol (Lee Dong-wook) is a competent police officer of the drug squad who only has a high school education. Due to his ambitions, he only takes on cases his superiors like and gives them the desired outcome, yet turning a blind eye to some injustice. As the promotion period nears, Soo-yeol with no backing is faced with the crisis of losing his opportunity once again. Congressman Do Yoo-gon gives Soo-yeol hope for a promotion again, but his life comes to complete halt when K (Wi Ha-joon) who is righteous but crazy, suddenly appears at the critical moment! What will the meeting of ‘bad guy’ Soo-yeol and the crazy guy, K, bring?
