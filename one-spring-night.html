One Spring Night

Title: 봄밤 / One Spring Night
Genre: Romance
Episodes: 32
Broadcast network: MBC
Broadcast period: 2019-May-22 to 2019-July-11
Air time: Wednesday & Thursday 22:00 KST

One Spring Night is a story of two people whose uneventful lives become shaken by unexpected love, leading them to seek what their hearts truly desire, instead of being fixated on faith. Lee Jung In is a librarian who knows how to appreciate the small things in life. Yu Ji Ho is a well-mannered pharmacist who tends not to hesitate when it comes to love.
