True Beauty

Title: 여신강림 / True Beauty
Genre: Romance, Comedy, School
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: tvN
Broadcast period: 2020-Dec-09 to 2021-Feb-04
Air time: Wednesday & Thursday 22:50 KST

Im Joo Kyung (Moon Ga Young) is a high school girl with a complex about her natural appearance. Watching online tutorials, she becomes a master of make-up. She is popular at school and is considered quite pretty by her classmates, but she is terrified to show anyone her bare face.
