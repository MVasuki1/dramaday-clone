Agency

Title: 대행사 / Agency
Genre: Office
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: JTBC
Broadcast period: 2023-Jan-07 to 2023-Feb-26
Air time: Saturday & Sunday 22:30 KST

Go Ah-in is the living icon of going from “rags-to-riches”. An orphan who grew up without her parents, she had to give up on entering a good college out of poverty. After working endlessly for 19 years, Ah-in proved her own worth and became the very first female board member of VC Group. While outsiders believe that to be her limit, Ah-in prepares to exceed expectations and begins to aim for the very top.
