Love Affairs in the Afternoon

Title: 평일 오후 세시의 연인 / Love Affairs in the Afternoon
Also known as: Weekday at 3PM Lover
Genre: Melodrama, Romance
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: Channel A
Broadcast period: 2019-July-05 to 2019-Aug-24
Air time: Friday & Saturday 23:00 KST

Son Ji Eun (Park Ha Sun) moves to a small city due to her husband’s new job as a civil servant. She works part-time at a mart. She is quiet and her life in this small town becomes boring. However, her boring life is changed after she meet Yoon Jung Woo (Lee Sang Yeob) who is a biology teacher at a local school.
