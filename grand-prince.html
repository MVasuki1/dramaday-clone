Grand Prince

Title: 대군 / 大君 / Grand Prince
Full Title: 대군 – 사랑을 그리다
Genre: Historical, Romance, Melodrama
Episodes: 20
Broadcast network: CSTV
Broadcast period: 2018-March-03 to 2018-May-06
Air time: Saturday & Sunday 22:50 KST

This drama is about a Love triangle between two brothers and the woman they love.

Lee Kang (Joo Sang Wook) is an ambitious prince. He falls in love with Sung Ja Hyun (Jin Se Yun). She is a beautiful woman from a prestigious family, but Lee Kang’s younger brother Lee Whi (Yoon Shi Yoon) also loves her. To have Ja Hyun, Lee Kang wants to become the King … even if he has to kill his younger brother.
