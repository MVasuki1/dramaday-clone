Our Beloved Summer

Title: 그 해 우리는 / Our Beloved Summer
Genre: Romance, Comedy
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: SBS
Broadcast period: 2021-Dec-06 to 2022-Jan-25
Air time: Monday & Tuesday 22:00 KST

A coming of age romantic comedy that revolves around Choi Woong (Choi Woo Shik) and Kook Yun Soo (Kim Da Mi), ex-lovers who broke up with a promise to never meet again. As luck would have it, the documentary they filmed ten years ago in high school went viral and they are forced to face the cameras together again. The series depicts their complicated feelings and growth.
