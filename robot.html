Air time: Wednesdays & Thursdays 22:00 (2 episodes back-to-back)

“I Am Not a Robot” is a romantic comedy that follows the story of a man who can’t date because he is allergic to people. He finds himself turning to a robot and falling in love, but the robot is actually a woman pretending to be one.

Main Cast

Yoo Seung Ho as Kim Min Kyu, Perfect from head to toe with an IQ of 157, Kim Min Kyu is head of Korea’s biggest financial company (KM), with such severe allergies that he lives in a sterile environment, devoid even of humans. However, there is a lot of mystery surrounding him, as he lives alone in a veil of residence, living alone in a mansion like a castle. He’s never had the experience of falling in love until he meets a girl robot one day

Chae Soo Bin as Jo Ji Ah / Aji 3, a passionate and energetic young entrepreneur who possesses an extremely high EQ (emotional intelligence) and whose dream is to make things that people need. Her ex-boyfriend Baek Gyun, a robotic engineer, asks her to act as a robot part-time.

Uhm Ki Joon as Hong Baek Gyun, Jo Ji Ah’s ex-boyfriend Hong Baek Gyun is a world-renown genius robotic engineer. In the drama, he secretly creates the world’s most advanced AI android robot “Aji3.” He is the man who is instrumental in Kim Mingyu finding love in the female robot. But the genius robot professor is a bumbling novice himself in the ways of romance.
