It’s Okay to Not Be Okay

Title: 사이코지만 괜찮아 / It’s Okay to Not Be Okay
Also known as: Psycho But It’s Okay
Genre: Romance, Fantasy
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: tvN
Broadcast period: 2020-Jun-20 to 2020-Aug-09
Air time: Saturday & Sunday 21:00 KST

It’s a “healing romance that resembles a storybook fantasy.” It tells the story of a community health worker at a psychiatric ward who doesn’t have time for love and a storybook writer who has never known love due to a birth defect. After meeting one another, the two slowly begin to heal each other’s emotional wounds.
