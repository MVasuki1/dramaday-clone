Mother

Title: 마더 / Mother
Also Known as: Call Me Mother
Genre: Melodrama
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: tvN
Broadcast period: 2018-Jan-24 to 2018-March-15
Air time: Wednesday & Thursday 21:30 KST

Hye Na (Heo Yool) is abused by her mother Ja Young (Go Sung Hee). Although she is not okay, she tells other people she is alright. However, Soo Jin (Lee Bo Young), a temporary teacher at Hye Na’s elementary school, becomes aware of her situation and impulsive decision to kidnap and become her mother.
