Title: 보이스 / Voice
Genre: Detective, Crime, Thriller
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: OCN
Broadcast period: 2017-Jan-14 to 2017-March-12
Air time: Saturday & Sunday 22:00 KST
Related series: Voice 2, Voice 3

Two detectives team up to catch a serial killer who murdered their family. Moo Jin Hyuk’s (Jang Hyuk) life spiraled out of control after his wife was murdered. He starts to put himself together after he meets Kang Kwon Joo (Lee Ha Na), US-graduated voice-profiler, who lost her police father to the same serial killer years before. They work together on the 112 (emergency telephone number) call center team.
