The Good Detective 2

Title: 모범형사 2 / The Good Detective 2
Genre: Detective, Crime , Investigation
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: jTBC
Broadcast period: 2022-July-30 to 2022-Sep-18
Air time: Monday & Tuesday 22:30 KST

Veteran detective Do-chang develops a trauma, after the man whom he had wrongly arrested lost his life on the death row. Now more timid than before, Do-chang considers switching to a desk job, but Ji-hyuk drags him back to the crime scene. Once someone who thinks of catching criminals as just a job, Ji-hyuk is now a compassionate detective who empathises with the victims.
