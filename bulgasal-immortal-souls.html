Bulgasal: Immortal Souls

Title: 불가살 / Bulgasal: Immortal Souls
Also known as: Immortality
Genre: Fantasy, Historical
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: tvN
Broadcast period: 2021-Dec-18 to 2022-Feb-06
Air time: Saturday & Sunday 21:00 KST

Story of a woman who repeats death and reincarnation as a human for 600 years and remembers her past lives, and a man who cannot kill nor be killed and has lived for the past 600 years as a bulgasal—a mythical creature that feeds off human blood and is cursed with immortality.
