Dark Hole

Title: 다크홀 / Dark Hole
Genre: Action, Thriller, Mystery, Sci-fi
Episodes: 12
Broadcast network: OCN
Broadcast period: 2021-Apr-30 to 2021-Jun-05
Air time: Friday & Saturday 22:30 KST

A black smoke comes out from a sinkhole and covers up a city called Muji. The city turns into a disaster, and people who breathe that dark smoke becomes dangerous monsters. Lee Hwa Sun, a detective who came from Seoul, starts to investigate this weird phenomenon and fights against the monsters with a wreck car driver Yoo Tae Han, who used to be an ex-detective in Muji.
