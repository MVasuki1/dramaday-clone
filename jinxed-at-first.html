Jinxed at First

Title: 징크스의 연인 / Jinxed at First
Also known as: Jinx’s Lover
Genre: Fantasy, Romance
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: KBS2
Broadcast period: 2022-June-15 to 2022-Aug-04
Air time: Wednesday & Thursday 21:30 KST

Adapted from the webtoon of the same name, the drama tells the story of a human man who regards his unfortunate life as his destiny and chooses to relent to it, and a goddess who jumps out of the unknown world to lift the curse. The two then escape their cruel fate together.
