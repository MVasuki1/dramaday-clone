Radio Romance

Title: 라디오 로맨스 / Radio Romance
Genre: Romance, Comedy
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: KBS2
Broadcast period: 2018-Jan-29 to 2018-March-20
Air time: Monday & Tuesday 22:00 KST

Soo Ho (Yoon Doo Joon) is a top class actor with a shining career. He debuted as a child actor, and at a very young age, he realized that in order to succeed, he must hide his true self behind a mask. Geu Rim (Kim So Hyun) is a young, passionate writer for a radio show. She is a woman with initiative and drive. Positive mindset and tenacity are her forte. Their story begins by Geu Rim casting Soo Ho for her radio show.
