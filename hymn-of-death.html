Hymn of Death

Title: 사의 찬미 / Hymn of Death
Also known as: Praise of Death / Death Song
Genre: Romance, Historical, Music
Episodes: 6
Broadcast network: SBS
Broadcast period: 2018-Nov-27 to 2019-Dec-04
Air time: Monday & Tuesday at 22:00 KST

The short drama is a remake of the award-winning 1991 film “Death Song” which based on the true story of Kim Woo Jin and Yoon Shim Duk.

It’s tells the tragic love story of Kim Woo Jin (Lee Jong Suk), a married stage drama writer, and Yoon Shim Duk (Shin Hye Sun), Korea’s first professional soprano, who meet while studying at Tokyo University in the 1920s. Yun Shim Duk’s recording of “Death Song” became the first Korean pop song in 1926.
