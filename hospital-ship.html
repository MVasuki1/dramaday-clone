Hospital Ship

Title: 병원선 / Hospital Ship
Genre: Medical, Romance
Episodes: 40 (35 minutes/episode)
Broadcast network: MBC
Broadcast period: 2017-Aug-30 to 2017-Nov-02
Air time: Wednesdays & Thursdays 22:00 KST (2 episodes back-to-back)

Hospital Ship centers on the lives of young medical doctors aboard a ship that travels to remote places where they provide medical services to the local inhabitants. The drama will highlight the development in their personal and career lives as they perform their duty as doctors.
