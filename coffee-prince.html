Coffee Prince

Title: 커피프린스 1호점 / 咖啡王子1号店 / Coffee Prince
Also known as: The 1st Shop of Coffee Prince
Genre: Romance, Comedy
Episodes: 17
Broadcast network: MBC
Broadcast period: 2007-Jul-02 to 2007-Aug-27
Air time: Monday and Tuesday 21:55 KST

This romantic comedy tells the story of Han Kyul (Gong Yoo), the handsome son of a wealthy hotelier family who is set in his bachelor ways and constantly deflects his family’s attempts to make him commit. The constant pressure to get married drives him to hire a goofy young delivery boy Eun Chan (Yoon Eun Hye) to pretend to be his gay lover to scare away his family’s set-ups. Trouble starts when Han Kyul begins to get to know the hardworking and lovable Eun Chan, and begins to develop real feelings for him—only to discover that “he” is actually a girl disguised as a boy.
