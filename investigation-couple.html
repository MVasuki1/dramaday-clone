Investigation Couple

Title: 검법남녀 / Investigation Couple
Also Known as: Partners for Justice
Genre: Mystery, Detective
Episodes: 32
Broadcast network: MBC
Broadcast period: 2018-May-14 to 2018-July-17
Air time: Monday and Tuesday 22:00 KST (2 episodes back-to-back)

Baek Beom (Jung Jae Young) has worked as a forensic doctor for 10 years, he is excellent with his work, but does not open his mind to other people. Eun Sol (Jung Yoo Mi) is a rookie prosecutor with a bright personality and she comes from wealth family background. The forensic scientist with a bad personality and a warm-hearted prosecutor must join forces in order to catch a serial killer.
