Sh**ting Stars

Title: 별똥별 / Sh**ting Stars
Also known as: Shooting Stars
Genre: Romance, Comedy
Episodes: 16
Broadcast network: tvN
Broadcast period: 2022-Apr-22 to 2022-June-11
Air time: Friday & Saturday 22:40 KST

Sh**ting Stars is about people in the entertainment industry who take care of top stars. They bleed, sweat, and shed tears to make their celebrities shine. A romance appears between the perfect and flawless top star and the head of the promotion team, who is at the forefront of the star management. This is about the sparkling stories of the two main characters and others surrounding them.
